Some Examples of Ellipse

Example: 1
(x2/a2) + (y2/b2) = 1

Here, the major axis is "x-axis" and minor axis is "y-axis"

( NOTE: We take the value of "a" = 4 and the value of "b" = 3)

Example: 2
(x2/b2) + (y2/a2) = 1

Here, the major axis is "y-axis" and minor axis is "x-axis"

( NOTE: We take the value of "a" = 5 and the value of "b" = 3)
